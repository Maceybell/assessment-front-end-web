My Neighborhood

Orem is strange because neighborhoods do not really have names. The city is just like a giant conglomerate of homes in the residential areas, with random streets sprinkles throughout that look nothing like all the other street around them. But if I had to give my neighborhood a name I would call it Cherry Hill, because many things nearby me are called Cherry Hill.
